Food Delivery App Redesign

Project Overview

This project is a reimagination of what a user’s interaction with the food delivery interface could be, carried out through wireframing and prototyping. We iterated through many different styles of design, ending with designing a food delivery app in the same way as a dating app.

Responsibilities

  • Lead and conduct user research
  • Prototype, wireframing and user testing
  • UX analysis and facilitating the design thinking process

Tools

  • Figma
  • Adobe Illustrator
  • Adobe Photoshop

Project Goals

Our primary goals were to:

  • Conduct user research to see what area of food delivery applications users would like to see different designs for
  • Wireframe and prototype a new user experience interface for users of food delivery applications
