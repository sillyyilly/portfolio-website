Website Design

Project Overview

I was commissioned to design the website homepage for a building development.

Responsibilities

  • Work with stakeholders to create a design that fulfills their needs and user needs

Tools

  • Figma
  • Canva
  • Adobe Photoshop

Design

I worked with a team of individuals, with other people responsible for the copywriting and implementation. I was in responsible for the design, and after researching what was important for stakeholders as well as users, I determined several requirements to design around.

  • Action call to register for development information
  • Description about the development
  • Ability to access floor plans
  • Smooth flow when scrolling for user experience
